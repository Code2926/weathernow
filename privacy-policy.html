Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 7, 2025

WeatherNow is developed by Muhammad Azan Ahmad (CodeNova). The app is designed to show current weather information based on your location.

Information Collection

WeatherNow only uses your device’s location to display accurate local weather. This location data is processed only on your device and is not stored, shared, or collected by the developer.

Third-Party Services

The app may access trusted weather APIs to fetch real-time weather data. These APIs may receive approximate location coordinates to provide weather updates, but no personal or identifiable information is shared.

Data Retention

WeatherNow does not store any personal data. All weather data is fetched in real time and discarded when you close the app.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
